Trip planning FAQ

Questions travellers ask before visiting Ghana

How many days do I need for a Ghana trip?

Five to seven days works for Accra, Cape Coast, and one nature escape. Ten to fourteen days gives enough time for Volta Region or Northern Ghana without rushing.

Can Local Insights plan a custom itinerary?

Yes. Share your dates, group size, budget range, comfort level, and interests. We can shape a private route around culture, food, nature, heritage, research, or group needs.

Is airport pickup available?

Airport arrival support can be included in custom plans, especially for first-time visitors, families, groups, and late arrivals.

What is the best time to visit Ghana?

Ghana can be visited year-round. Many travellers prefer the drier months for easier road travel and outdoor experiences, while festival-focused trips depend on specific dates.
